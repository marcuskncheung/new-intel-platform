Intelligence Management Platform

The all-in-one solution for secure, efficient, and professional intelligence case management.

  • Secure, role-based access for team
  • Multi-source intelligence: Email, WhatsApp, Patrol, Surveillance
  • Fast search, smart dashboard, and profile management
  • Assessment, scoring, and export for reporting
  • Attachments, files, and case documentation
  • 24/7 automated monitoring of the intelligence inbox for new emails
Main Features
  • Alleged Subject List: Visualize and manage all alleged subjects and their cases.
  • Search: Instantly find cases or subjects by name or agent number.
  • Intel Source: Centralize intelligence from Email, WhatsApp, Patrol, and Surveillance.
  • Profile Management: Create/edit profiles, upload files, and manage attachments.
  • Export: Download data to Excel (.xlsx) for reporting and analysis.
  • Authentication: Secure login and signup for authorized access.
How to Use
  1. Log in or sign up for an account.
  2. Use the Alleged Subject List or Search to find or create profiles.
  3. Go to Intel Source to manage intelligence from all channels.
  4. Assess intelligence entries using the provided forms and save your assessments.
  5. Export data as needed for further processing.
Tip: Use the navigation bar to quickly access all platform features.
Platform Overview

Intelligence Management Platform is built for professionals who need to handle sensitive intelligence from multiple sources in a secure, organized, and efficient way.

  • All data is securely stored and accessible only to authorized users.
  • Assessment forms and scoring help you prioritize and track cases.
  • Attachments and files are managed per case for easy reference.
  • Export options let you generate reports for further analysis.
